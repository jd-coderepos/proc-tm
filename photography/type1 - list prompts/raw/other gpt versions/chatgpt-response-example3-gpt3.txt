Certainly! Based on the given context, here is a detailed list of the steps for registering the Picture Style:

Step 1:

   - Select [Picture Style].
   - Under the [2] tab, choose [Picture Style] and press the <0> key.
   - The Picture Style selection screen will appear.
   
Step 2:

   - Select [User Def.].
   - Choose [User Def. *] and press the <B> button.
   - The Detail set. screen will appear.
   
Step 3:

   - Press <0>.
   - With [Picture Style] selected, press <0>.
   
Step 4:

   - Select the base Picture Style.
   - Press the <V> key to choose the desired base Picture Style.
   - Press <0>.
   - If you already have a Picture Style set with the provided software, select it here.
   
Step 5:

   - Select a parameter.
   - Choose a specific parameter such as [Sharpness].
   - Press <0>.
   
Step 6:

   - Set the parameter.
   - Press the <U> key to adjust the parameter as desired.
   - Press <0>.
   - For more details, refer to "Customizing the Picture Style" on pages 82-84.
   
Step 7:

   - Press the <M> button to register the new Picture Style.
   - The Picture Style selection screen will reappear.
   - The base Picture Style will be indicated on the right of [User Def. *].
   - The name of the Picture Style with modified settings (different from the default) will be displayed in blue.
   - Note: Changing the base Picture Style in Step 4 will nullify the parameter settings of the registered Picture Style.
