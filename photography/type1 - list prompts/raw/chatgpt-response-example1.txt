1. Select [Format]:
	- Under the [5] tab, select [Format], then press <0>.

2. Format the card:
	- Select [OK], then press <0>.
	- The card will be formatted.
	- When the formatting is completed, the menu will reappear.
	- For low-level formatting:
		> Press the <L> button.
		> Checkmark [Low level format] with <X>, then select [OK].

3. Completion:
	- The card has been formatted.

Please note that if the card is new or was previously formatted by another camera or personal computer, it's advised to format the card with the camera. Formatting will erase everything on the card, including protected images. Ensure there's nothing you need to keep on the card, and if necessary, transfer the images to a personal computer before formatting.
