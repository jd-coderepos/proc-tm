Yes, the detailed list of steps for formatting the card procedure in the given context is as follows:

1. Select the [Format] option.
   - Go to the [5] tab.
   - Select [Format].
   - Press <0>.

2. Format the card.
   - Select [OK].
   - Press <0>.

3. Wait for the card to be formatted.
   - The card will be formatted.
   - When the formatting is completed, the menu will reappear.

4. (Optional) Perform a low-level format.
   - Press the <L> button.
   - Checkmark [Low level format] with <X>.
   - Select [OK].

It is important to note that formatting the card will erase all data, including protected images. Therefore, it is recommended to transfer any important data to a personal computer before formatting the card.
