1. Remove Pins:
	- Remove the spring locking pin (A) and drilled pin (B) on each side of the ROPS.
2. Lower the Crossbar:
	- Carefully lower the ROPS crossbar (C) about halfway down.
3. Install Pins:
	- Install the drilled pins (B) and spring locking pins (A).
	- Lower the crossbar (C) onto the drilled pins (B).
