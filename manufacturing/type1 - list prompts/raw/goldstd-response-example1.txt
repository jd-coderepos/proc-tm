Pump Installation Steps:

1. Pre-installation Precautions:
   a. Ensure proper motor certification in explosive environments.
   b. Ground all equipment to prevent discharge, damage, shock, and injury.
   c. Verify correct connection of ground lead.
   d. Electrical connections by certified electricians following regulations.
   e. Recommend supervision by authorized ITT representative for installation.

2. Inspection of the Pump:
   a. Remove plastic shipping plugs from vent holes in head column and casing.
      - Location: "A" as indicated in Figure 6.
   b. Take out all equipment from shipping containers.
   c. Thoroughly clean underside of support plate and both sides of optional pit cover.
   d. Remove grease from machined surfaces.
